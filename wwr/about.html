At Puebla Iztaccihuatl Rafting Company we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgetble rafting adventures. We are engaged with our clients and employees.

History

The company was stablished in 1989 at first to preserve a big nature park at the mountains of Sierra Nevada. Then, the company offered recreational trips and camping. In the present, the company offers a great deal of recreational services, and creates nature preservation programs in the location.
